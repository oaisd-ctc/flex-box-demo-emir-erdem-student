Flex-Wrap

The flex-wrap property allows items, (especially those nested in divs), to not fall outside of the border/boundary and instead wrap or fall down into the next line to stay within the boundaries. Try by zooming in.

Flex-Nowrap

Contrary to flex wrap, nowrap means that regardless of the zooming the items that are nested will not collapse and will fall outside the boundaries.

Flex Item Order

Using the flex item order property you are able to use an inline style with a declaration that reads "order: [number]" which places that nested div in whichever order you declared it.
In this case I applied "order: 3" to my div numbered 5, which placed it in 3rd shown below.

Flex Item Grow

The flex grow property allows you to determine how much a certain item grows in relation to the others. In this case number 2 and 5 grow a ton when zooming out.
Even when zoomed in a bit, you can realize that they are bigger than others.
